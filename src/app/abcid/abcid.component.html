How to Create Your ABC ID

Your Academic Bank of Credits (ABC) ID is essential for storing and accessing your academic credits. Follow the steps below to create your ABC ID easily.

Step 1: Visit the ABC Website

Go to the official portal: www.abc.gov.in

Step 2: Click on “Create ABC ID”

You will find the button on the homepage under the Student Services section.

Step 3: Select Authentication Method

  • Aadhaar Number
  • Mobile OTP
  • DigiLocker Account

Step 4: Fill Your Basic Details

Enter your Name, DOB, Mobile Number, and Email as per Aadhaar records.

Step 5: Verify Your Identity

Complete OTP verification to confirm your identity.

Step 6: Create Your ABC ID

Your unique 12-digit ABC ID will be generated. Save it safely.

Step 7: Login to ABC Dashboard

Use your ABC ID to sign in and view your academic credits.

Note: Use the same mobile number that is linked to your Aadhaar for smooth verification.
