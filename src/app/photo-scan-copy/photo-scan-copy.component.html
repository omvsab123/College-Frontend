SPPU Photocopy & Revaluation Application

Students who wish to apply for *Photocopy* or *Revaluation* of their answer sheets can follow the steps below. Make sure you apply within the official SPPU timeline.

üìÑ What is Photocopy?

Photocopy allows you to get a scanned copy of your evaluated answer sheet. This helps you understand your marking and verify if any question is unchecked.

üìù What is Revaluation?

Revaluation means re-checking your answer sheet by a different examiner. You can apply for revaluation *only after receiving your photocopy*.

üìå Steps to Apply for Photocopy / Revaluation

Step 1: Open SPPU Photocopy Portal

Click the button below to visit the official SPPU website.

Step 2: Login / Register

Use your PRN, mobile number, and email ID to register your account.

Step 3: Select Exam & Subject

Choose your exam month, year, and select the subject you want photocopy for.

Step 4: Pay Fees Online

Pay the required amount using UPI, credit/debit card, or net banking.

Step 5: Download Photocopy

Your answer sheet photocopy will be available within a few days.

Step 6: Apply for Revaluation (Optional)

If needed, apply for revaluation after checking your photocopy carefully.

Note: Revaluation without applying for photocopy is not allowed.
