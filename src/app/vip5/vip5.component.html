Message from the Principal

I am delighted to welcome you to Padmashri Manibhai Desai College, an institution firmly anchored in the Gandhian values of education and rural development.

Our college stands in the historic village of Uruli Kanchan, a place blessed by the visit of Mahatma Gandhi and known for its enduring commitment to self-reliance, simplicity and social transformation.

Our founder, Padmashri Manibhai Desai, was a visionary leader and a true disciple of Mahatma Gandhi. His life’s mission was to bring education, progress and dignity to rural India. Inspired by his ideals, our college strives to blend academic excellence with moral and social responsibility, nurturing students who are intellectually capable and socially conscious.

In tune with the guiding principles of the NEP 2020, our college is dedicated to providing a holistic, multidisciplinary and inclusive education that empowers learners to become innovators, critical thinkers and responsible citizens. Through experiential learning, creativity and skill development we prepare students for the opportunities and challenges of the 21st century.

At Padmashri Manibhai Desai College, we believe that education is not merely a path to personal success but a means to serve society. Upholding the legacy of our founder, we continue to promote the values of hard work, compassion and rural upliftment through education.

I warmly invite students, parents and the community to join us in our collective mission — to enlighten minds, empower lives and build a better tomorrow grounded in the timeless values of truth, service and humanity.

Prof. (Dr.) Shivaji Borhade
Principal
